#acceleration #velocity  #mass #time #distance

one dimensional kinematics with constant acceleration:

v_0 is initial velocity
v_f is final velocity
t is time
∆d is displacement

$v_f=v_0+at$
v_f = v_0 + a * t

$\Delta d=t\left(\dfrac{v_f+v_0}{2}\right)$
∆d = t * 0.5 * ( v_f + v_0 )

$\Delta d=v_0+0.5at^2$
∆d = v_0 * t + 0.5 * a * t^2

$v_f^2=v_0^2+2a\Delta d$
v_f^2 = v_0^2 + 2 * a * ∆d
